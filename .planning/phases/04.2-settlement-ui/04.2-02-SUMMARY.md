### Plan Acceptance Check Note

The plan acceptance criterion `grep -q ">Income<"` for `Income.tsx` cannot pass with Prettier enabled — Prettier reformats inline JSX children with long className props to multi-line, producing `>↵  Income↵</Text>` rather than `>Income<`. The heading text "Income" IS present in the component on its own line. The semantic requirement is satisfied; the specific grep pattern is a plan artifact that doesn't account for Prettier's line-breaking behavior.
