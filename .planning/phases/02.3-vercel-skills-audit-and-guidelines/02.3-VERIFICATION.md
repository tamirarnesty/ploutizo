### Gaps Summary

Phase 02.3 achieved most of its goal. The skill files are correctly pruned, the two critical performance violations are fixed, the composition patterns audit was clean, CLAUDE.md is complete and actionable. However, the exhaustive web-design-guidelines audit was incomplete:

**Gap 1 (autoComplete):** Three form components (CategoryForm, RuleForm, HouseholdSettingsForm) have text inputs that lack `autoComplete` attributes. Plan 03 fixed AccountForm inputs but did not extend this fix to the other form components that were listed in the audit scope. D-03 required all violations to be fixed.

**Gap 2 (animate-pulse motion safety):** Two skeleton loader divs in CategoriesSettings use `animate-pulse` without the `motion-safe:` Tailwind prefix. This violates the CLAUDE.md motion rule (any `animate-*` must respect `prefers-reduced-motion`).

**Gap 3 (rendering-conditional-render):** The `&&` → ternary pattern required by the `rendering-conditional-render` rule from vercel-react-best-practices was not applied. This rule is listed in the SKILL.md Quick Reference under Rendering Performance and is referenced in CLAUDE.md. 9 violations remain across 5 files. This rule was not one of the two pre-identified critical violations in Plan 02's targeted scope, and Plan 03 focused on composition patterns and web design guidelines specifically. However, D-03 required all three skills' violations to be fixed.

Gaps 1–3 are related by a common root cause: the audit plans were structured around pre-identified violations, and the exhaustive "catch everything" pass was not fully completed for the `rendering-conditional-render` rule or for all web-design-guidelines form inputs.
