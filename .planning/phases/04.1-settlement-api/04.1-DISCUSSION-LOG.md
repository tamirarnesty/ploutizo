## Settlement Concept Reframe (major mid-discussion pivot)

**User message:** "I think we need to reframe the settlement piece. the point of settling isn't exclusively across people... we're in a household, I'm not gonna owe emily directly. just what my txns were across mine, hers, or shared cards."

**Result:** Dropped `pairBalances` and `netLines` entirely from the API response. Final response: `{ accounts: [...] }` only. Cross-member summary can be derived client-side if needed.

**User follow-up on eachPersonPaysOwn:** "for the shared card portion, I still owe the card my portion of the balance - it's just based on a split of a txn... essentially, every transaction on every card can be 'personal' (one owner, full txn amount) and 'shared' (multiple owners, split amount of transaction that sums to full amount)"

**User follow-up dropping eachPersonPaysOwn:** "I think we need to drop this flag then entirely. it doesn't make sense for what I want. I will always be paying a card, not a person."

**Result:** `eachPersonPaysOwn` field dropped from schema and accounts API in this phase. All accounts participate in settlement equally.
